    Landslide Education

    What causes landslides?

    • Heavy rainfall
    • Steep slopes
    • Weak or clay soil
    • Low vegetation
    • Earthquakes or human activities

    Safety Tips:

    • Avoid building near steep slopes
    • Stay alert during heavy rainfall
    • Plant vegetation to stabilize soil
    • Follow evacuation warnings
